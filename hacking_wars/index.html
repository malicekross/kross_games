OBJECTIVE

Overwhelm the enemy system (Red) with your packets (Green). Reduce their score to 0 to win.

CONTROLS

  • Connect: Drag from your node to another to stream packets.
  • Cut Wire: Drag across an existing wire to sever it.
  • Reverse: Drag a wire in the opposite direction to swap it.

SYSTEM SPECS

  • Level 1: 1 Wire Max. Speed: 1.1x
  • Level 2: (15 Packets) 2 Wires Max. Speed: 2.2x
  • Level 3: (30 Packets) 3 Wires Max. Speed: 3.3x

GAMEPLAY

  • Nodes: Numbers show (Current / Max) packets.
  • Overflow: Full nodes automatically forward packets.
  • Capture: Enemy packets reduce node value. Reaching 0 captures the node.
